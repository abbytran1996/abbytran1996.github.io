RecruitR V.3

RecruitR is an app designed to match students to jobs that best fit their skill sets, with an emphasis on application visibility for students, and application processing speed for recruiters. With RecruitR’s automated matching system, students will be matched to jobs based on skills, experience, preferences, and more to provide a robust initial match.


After automated matching, a short three-phase application process provides quick turn around for students and recruiters to find the perfect student-to-job match in a fraction of the time compared to traditional resume applications. Recruiters can bulk manage their students by different filters. Students can also review the list of matches and work on multiple processes at the same time.


The app will be avaiable on both Andoird and iOs platforms.
